## Overview

The definitive primary source on [[omegawiki]] — README, repo tree, CLAUDE.md, and the author's own gist comments across April-May 2026. Crucially, it **resolves the earlier contradiction** about OmegaWiki's typed-graph size by showing the figures *changed across versions*.

## The contradiction, resolved

Earlier this wiki recorded a conflict between the [[research-report]] ("9×9") and the May gist thread ("8 entities / 20 edges"). The primary repo shows both are right at different times: the **April 13** thread said 8 entity types / 9 relationship types / 20 skills; **mid-May** comments said 8 entities / 20 edges / 26 skills; the **current README** says 9 entity types / 9 relationship types / 23 skills. The counts are a moving target tied to releases — there is no single canonical "N×M." This is itself a small lesson in the [[lossy-summarization-critique]]: a static figure copied into a page goes stale unless dated.
