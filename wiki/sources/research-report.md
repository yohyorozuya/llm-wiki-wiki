# LLM Wiki Expert Briefing (research report)

## Overview

The primary source that seeded this wiki: a comprehensive briefing on [[llm-wiki-pattern]], compiled May 2026 from Karpathy's original gist, its comment thread, the GitHub repos of community implementations, and secondary analyses. It is the source-of-truth document for nearly every claim in this knowledge base. Fittingly, this wiki is itself an LLM Wiki — a demonstration of the pattern it documents.

## What it covers

The report spans the original methodology (three layers, three operations), conceptual foundations ([[compilation-vs-retrieval]], [[llm-wiki-vs-rag]]), the full ecosystem of the implementations, critiques and open problems (the [[three-failure-modes]], the [[transactional-overhead-critique]]), reception, and advanced variations ([[hybrid-retrieval]], [[tiered-memory]], [[typed-knowledge-graph]], [[routing-layer]]).
