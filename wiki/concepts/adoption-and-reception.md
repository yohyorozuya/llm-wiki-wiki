# Adoption and Reception

## Overview

How the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] was received. The [[research-report]] cited "5,000+ stars" and ~16M tweet views in 48 hours; the primary [[karpathy-gist|gist]] confirms the display caps at "5,000+" but late-May 2026 search metadata shows the gist at roughly **33,940 stars / 7,226 forks / 845 comments** — an order of magnitude beyond the report's figure, and a sign of how fast adoption ran.

## The comment thread as a living artifact

The gist's own comment thread became a rolling showcase of implementations (see [[comment-thread-implementations]]) — so much so that it doubles as an informal registry of the ecosystem. New tools shipped weekly through April and May 2026.
