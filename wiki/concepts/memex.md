# Memex (1945)

## Overview

Vannevar Bush's 1945 vision of a personal, curated knowledge store with associative trails between documents — the intellectual ancestor of the [[llm-wiki-pattern]]. Karpathy: "Bush's vision was closer to this than to what the web became: private, actively curated, with the connections between documents as valuable as the documents themselves. The part he couldn't solve was who does the maintenance. The LLM handles that."

## Cross-references
