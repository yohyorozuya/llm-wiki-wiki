# Andrej Karpathy

## Overview

The originator of the [[llm-wiki-pattern]], which he published April 4, 2026 as an abstract "idea file" gist. Co-founder of OpenAI and former Director of AI at Tesla. His motivating observation: "a large fraction of my recent token throughput is going less into manipulating code, and more into manipulating knowledge."

## Contributions to the pattern

The three-layer architecture, the three operations, the "idea file" meta-genre, and the framing slogan "Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase." Also authored the companion HELLO.md gist on agent identity without continuity.
